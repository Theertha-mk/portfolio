PROJECT

IOT BASED HOME AUTOMATION
  • This project explores numerous possibilities for interlinking smart devices while making judicious use of wireless fidelity (Wi-Fi) technology and internet of things (IOT).
  • The system helps in real time monitoring of electric parameters like power, voltage, and current.
  • It stores these data for a period of one month over internet. It also helps in motion sensing and detection of gas leakage.
  • It is possible to connect our system with Google service and Amazon Alexa. This enables us to control the devices using voice
  • it provides continuous speed regulation of fan without energy loss. .
  • the main control unit of our system is ESP8266 Wi-Fi module which has an inbuilt Wi-Fi facility.
  • The microcontroller is connected with Adafruit-IO (web cloud) platform through internet and the communication between the ESP8266 and Adafruit-IO takes place with the help of MQTT (Message Queuing Telemetry Transport).
